RESIDENTIAL DESIGN

Project brief:

The house library design exudes a cozy yet sophisticated ambiance, seamlessly blending functionality with aesthetic appeal. The space is organized with shelves that maximize storage while maintaining an inviting atmosphere. Thoughtfully placed seating areas, including armchairs and reading nooks, create comfortable spots for relaxation and immersion in books. RECREATIONAL REDEVELOPMENT

Project brief:

The park design showcases a harmonious blend of modern architecture and natural landscaping, creating an inviting and functional recreational space. The first area features curved pathways bordered by lush greenery and integrated seating arrangements, ideal for relaxation or small gatherings. Adjacent to this is a vibrant children's play zone equipped with colorful slides, climbing frames, swings, and built-in trampolines, all designed with safety and engagement in mind. The design includes open, grassy areas and thoughtfully placed trees, adding shade and aesthetic balance. With its contemporary structures and dynamic spaces, the park fosters interaction, play, and relaxation for visitors of all ages.

Project brief:
