Services

What i do

Renovation and Remodeling

Breathing new life into existing spaces is our forte. I reimagine layouts and elements, transforming the old into the extraordinary while preserving the integrity of the original design vision.

Consultations

Sometimes all you need is a guiding voice. My consultation services provide expert insights and tailored recommendations, empowering you to make informed design choices even on a smaller scale.

Styling and Staging

For residential properties, I infuse the art of staging into the equation. Our magic transforms spaces into captivating showcases, maximizing their market appeal and leaving a lasting impression.

Space Planning

I meticulously analyze spaces to unlock their full potential.I craft layouts that seamlessly blend functionality with aesthetics, ensuring every square foot is optimized for purpose and flow.

Project Management

Seamlessly orchestrating the symphony of a project is where I thrive. From coordinating contractors to ensuring each design element aligns with the grand picture, I meticulously manage every detail.

Sourcing and Procurement

My extensive network opens doors to exclusive resources. I help in source furnishings, materials, and decor that aren't just beautiful but embody the essence of your design, enhancing the uniqueness of your space.
